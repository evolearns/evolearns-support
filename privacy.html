Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2025

Resume AI Enhancer (“the App”), operated by EvoLearns (“we,” “us,” or “our”), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Resume and Job Description Text: The text you paste into the App is used solely to perform analysis, scoring, and enhancements.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect basic diagnostic logs (crash logs, performance logs) to improve reliability.
  • Device Information: Non-identifying device metadata such as operating system version, device type, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze resumes and job descriptions and provide enhanced results.
  • To generate suggestions, improvements, and ATS-style scoring.
  • To improve app performance, detect crashes, and enhance reliability.
  • To maintain security and prevent misuse.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Resume text and job descriptions may be processed temporarily to generate your results. We do not sell your data. Some anonymized logs may be retained to improve service reliability.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We do not share your resume content with third-party advertisers. Data may be shared with trusted service providers only for:

  • Error diagnostics and crash reporting
  • Secure data processing
  • App performance monitoring

All service providers are obligated to protect your data and cannot sell or misuse it.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request:

  • Deletion of your data
  • Access to any data stored about you
  • Correction of inaccurate information

To exercise these rights, contact us at: ir@evolearns.com

6. Data Protection

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit, secure servers, strict access control, and continuous monitoring.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, contact us immediately.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. When updated, the “Last updated” date will change. Continued use of the App means you accept the revised Policy.
