App Overview

Resume AI Enhancer - EvoLearns helps you optimize your resume against a target job description.

  • Paste your resume and a job posting.
  • Get an ATS-style score and gap analysis.
  • Generate an improved, best-version resume while staying truthful to your experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you store my resume or job descriptions?

Resumes and job descriptions may be processed by our backend to provide scoring and enhancements. We do not sell your data.

Is my data shared with third parties?

Your data is used only to provide resume analysis and enhancements in the app. We do not sell or trade your personal information.

What should I do if I see an error in the app?

Take a screenshot if possible and email us with a short description of what you were doing when the error occurred.
